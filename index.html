Accelerating Economic Equality

ChekOut tokens (CHEKS) are digital tokens that can only be used to purchase goods and services from businesses owned by people of color. 

Backed by Cryptocurrency

All ChekOut tokens (CHEKS) are backed by top-performing cryptocurrencies. All crypto holdings can be audited by the public at anytime.

Educate. Empower. Liberate.

20% of all ChekOut tokens (CHEKS) proceeds are used to educate and uplift communities of color, both financially and technologically.

Redistribution of Wealth

CHEKS can only be spent at businesses owned by people of color. This creates an ecosystem in which all CHEKS, and by default the crypto backings it holds, stays in the communities it serves.

Backed by Cryptocurrency

CHEKS are fully backed by
cryptocurrencies. The value of CHEKS will adjust based on the value of the cryptocurrencies that are backing it. Holdings are mostly comprised of Ethereum and Bitcoin.

Fast. Secure. Trustless. 

All transactions are peer-to-peer. There is no middle man or entity that needs to approve any transaction whatsoever. Payments are typically under 30 seconds and are securely done using blockchain technology.
